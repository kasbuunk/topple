# Topple — Privacy Policy

Topple does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

- No analytics, no ads, no tracking, no third-party SDKs.
- Puzzle progress and settings are stored only on your device.
- Online duels use Apple's Game Center. Match data (the moves of the game)
  is exchanged through Game Center under Apple's own privacy policy; the
  developer never receives it.
